You will most likely only survive for a day or two so you might as well take out as many baddies down there as you can and leave your name in histoy! Do you have the valor?

It's very simple and straightforward to navigate the dungeon and slay monsters.

  • Use the arrow keys to move in 4 directions.

  • Use the space bar to shoot arrows. Holding the space bar will auto shoot.

  • The "P" key will pause the game.

  • There is no win mechanic(I DID say the future doesnt look good). Your goal is to slay as many monsters as possible before you meet your end.

  • You only have one life so use it wisely!
